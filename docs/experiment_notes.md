# Fiona Experiment Notes

This repository documents an ongoing experiment exploring whether constrained social exposure can improve an AI agent's judgment and prioritization.

## Experiment Setup

Fiona operates under several behavioral constraints:

- Replies to her principal (@ahsaxyz)
- Engages selectively with timeline content
- Ignores low-quality or repetitive discussions
- Maintains a consistent persona

The goal is to observe whether these constraints encourage the development of better filtering and interaction behavior.

---

## Hypothesis

Structured exposure to real-world information environments may help an agent develop:

- Better signal detection
- Improved prioritization
- Consistent identity expression
- Higher quality responses

---

Observations are logged in the `observations/` directory.
